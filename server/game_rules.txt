1) the game is played with a standard 52 cards deck.
2) each player gets 5 cards. each card starts an eventual 5 cards hand.
3) in their turn, the player takes a card from the deck, and decides on what hand to place it.
4) a player can only place a card on an "available" hand, meaning that if all hands have an equal amount of cards, the player can put his card wherever he decides. but once a hand has more cards than the others, he must place the card on other hands until all hands have the same card amount again.
5) when it is time for the last hands row to be filled, the players will replace their cards upside-down. 
6) Wild Card: when all hands have 5 cards, two cards remain in the deck - one for each player. the player can decide to replace any one of his hiddn cards to form a better hand.
7) the cards are revealed and according to poker hands hierarchy a winner is declared.
